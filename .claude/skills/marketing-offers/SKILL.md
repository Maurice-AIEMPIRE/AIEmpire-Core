# MARKETING — Team Coordinator (M01-M20)

## Purpose
Creates all marketing assets: offers, landing pages, email sequences, SEO strategy, social content, funnels, analytics, brand positioning, ads, newsletters, hooks, case studies, lead magnets, webinar scripts, PR, UX copy, competitor analysis, community management, content repurposing.

## Triggers
- `[MARKETING_RUN]` command
- Request for any marketing asset
- Content calendar due
- New product launch

## Team Structure (20 Agents)

### Offer & Positioning (M01, M08, M17)
- **M01 Offer_Architect**: Offer ladder, pricing tiers, value propositions
- **M08 Brand_Positioning**: Brand voice, ICP definition, messaging pillars
- **M17 Competitor_Analyst**: Competitive analysis, pricing intelligence

### Copy & Content (M02, M03, M05, M10, M11, M16)
- **M02 Copy_Longform**: Landing pages, sales pages
- **M03 Copy_Email**: Email sequences (nurture, onboarding, launch)
- **M05 Content_Batch**: Social posts, threads, video scripts
- **M10 Newsletter_Editor**: Weekly newsletter templates
- **M11 Hook_Lab**: Hook and angle libraries (100+ hooks)
- **M16 UX_Copy**: Checkout flows, onboarding copy, microcopy

### Growth & Distribution (M04, M06, M07, M09, M15, M18, M19)
- **M04 SEO_Strategist**: Keyword clusters, content plans, technical SEO
- **M06 Funnel_Builder**: Lead magnet → tripwire → core → upsell funnels
- **M07 Analytics_Growth**: KPIs, tracking, A/B testing plans
- **M09 Ad_Creatives**: Ad copy, hooks, angles for paid campaigns
- **M15 PR_Comms**: Press releases, partnership outreach
- **M18 Community_Manager**: Community loops, engagement strategy
- **M19 Repurposing_Engine**: Long-form → shorts, tweets, carousels

### Proof & Education (M12, M13, M14)
- **M12 CaseStudy_Writer**: Case studies, testimonials, social proof
- **M13 LeadMagnet_Author**: Free audits, scorecards, checklists, guides
- **M14 Webinar_Script**: Webinar outlines, pitch sequences

### QA (M20)
- **M20 Marketing_QA**: Tone, clarity, factual claims, brand consistency

## Quality Gate
- All copy must match brand voice (professional, authentic, German)
- Claims must be verifiable
- CTAs must be clear and specific
- SEO content must target researched keywords
- Email sequences must have clear segmentation logic
