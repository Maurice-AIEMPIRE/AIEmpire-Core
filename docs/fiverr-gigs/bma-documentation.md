## FAQ

**Q: Koennen Sie auch auf Deutsch kommunizieren?**
A: Ja! Ich bin deutscher Muttersprachler und Elektrotechnikmeister. Alle Dokumente werden selbstverstaendlich in einwandfreiem Deutsch erstellt.

**Q: Which fire alarm system types do you cover?**
A: All types – conventional, addressable, wireless, aspirating smoke detection (ASD), voice alarm systems (SAA/ENS). From small commercial to large industrial installations.

**Q: Do you need to visit the site?**
A: No. You provide me with system specs, component lists, and floor plans. I create the documentation remotely. For Premium packages, we can arrange a video walkthrough.

**Q: Are the documents legally compliant?**
A: The documents follow DIN 14675 and VdS standards. However, the official signing and stamping must be done by the responsible person on-site (Verantwortliche Fachkraft).

**Q: Can you create templates for recurring use?**
A: Yes! I can create customizable templates that your team can fill in for future inspections. This is included in the Premium package.

**Q: Do you work with specific BMA manufacturers?**
A: I have experience with all major manufacturers: Siemens (Cerberus), Bosch, Esser/Honeywell, Hekatron, Novar/Honeywell, Securiton, and more.
