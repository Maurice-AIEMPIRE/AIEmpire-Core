If our collected data shows:
Aggressive Copy's conversion rate is statistically significant (> 1.5 times polite copy) and meets or exceeds an established baseline from historical performance metrics, then we'll conclude the hypothesis as True (H0 rejected).

then we may have insufficient evidence either way.
