# Revenue

Financial tracking, reports, and revenue data.

## Purpose
- Track revenue across all channels (Gumroad, Stripe, Fiverr, Consulting)
- Store financial reports and projections
- Monitor KPIs and conversion metrics

## Files
- **monthly/** - Monthly revenue reports
- **channels/** - Per-channel revenue tracking
- **projections/** - Revenue forecasts and targets
- **expenses/** - Cost tracking (API costs, tools, services)
